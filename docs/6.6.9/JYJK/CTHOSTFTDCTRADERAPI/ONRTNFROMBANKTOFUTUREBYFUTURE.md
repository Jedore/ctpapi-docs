## 1.说明
<p>期货发起银行资金转期货通知，当执行<a href="../../CTHOSTFTDCTRADERSPI/REQFROMBANKTOFUTUREBYFUTURE/">ReqFromBankToFutureByFuture</a>后并且报出后，收到返回则调用此接口，私有流回报。</p>
<span class="anchor" id="065e64c1-a092-46e7-bc2b-225bcc5e7397"></span>
## 2.函数原型
<p>virtual void OnRtnFromBankToFutureByFuture(CThostFtdcRspTransferField *pRspTransfer) {};</p>
